Our Mission

Our organization inspires citizens of developing nations to make a positive difference in their communities. We focus on providing free technological resources to schools, entrepreneurs, and community groups. These tools empower young minds change their quality of life by allowing users to access more knowledge and develop literacy skills. Technology is an important part of the 21st century. Everyone should have the opportunity to learn and expand their technological abilities.

Schools

We will improve education by giving students the ability to learn with technology. As well as the benefits from the shared knowledge given by other countries.

Entrepreneurs

By expanding the technical abilities of a society, we create an atmosphere for business, jobs, and a better quality of life.

Community Groups

We will help community by donating computers to local libraries which will enable people of all ages in the community to lean and access more opportunities.
